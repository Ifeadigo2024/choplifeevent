FAQs

Quick answers to the most common questions.

How early should I book?

For weddings and large events, we recommend booking as early as possible. For smaller events, a few weeks can work depending on your needs and date.

Do you handle vendors?

Yes. We can source, coordinate, and manage vendors based on your budget and style - or work with vendors you already have.

Can you do decor only?

Absolutely. We offer decor and venue setup as a standalone service, including styling direction, setup, and teardown.
